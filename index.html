About Me

I'm a sophomore at Northwestern University pursuing a double major in Biomedical Engineering and Computer Science (3.91 GPA, Dean's List). My work focuses on autonomous robotics, embedded systems, and surgical robotics.

Currently, I serve as Software Lead for Northwestern Space Technology And Rocketry Society (NUSTARS), developing autonomous navigation systems for NASA Lunabotics competition rovers. I'm also involved with the Motorcats Baja SAE team, working on electronics and mechanical systems for off-road racing vehicles.

My interest in surgical robotics stems from early exposure to medical device development during my internship at Hypervision Surgical, a UK-based medical technology startup specializing in hyperspectral imaging for surgical applications.
