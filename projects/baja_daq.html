Baja SAE Data Acquisition System

Northwestern Motorcats | Electronics Team

Overview

Designing a data acquisition and telemetry system for the Motorcats Baja SAE competition vehicle. The system will monitor wheel speeds in real-time, enabling data-driven analysis of drivetrain performance and vehicle dynamics during testing and competition.

This is the team's first dedicated DAQ system, replacing manual observation with quantitative data collection.

System Design

The system is built around an ESP32 microcontroller, chosen for its dual-core processor, and low power consumption. The architecture prioritizes reliability in harsh off-road conditions.

  • Wheel Speed Sensing: Four hall-effect sensors (one per wheel) detect magnetic targets on the wheel hubs, providing pulse signals proportional to wheel rotation speed.
  • Signal Conditioning: Custom PCB with input protection, pull-up resistors, and filtering to clean up sensor signals before they reach the microcontroller.
  • Local Logging: SD card storage for high-frequency data capture, allowing detailed post-run analysis.

PCB Design

Designed in KiCad with focus on noise immunity and manufacturability:

  • Separate analog and digital ground planes to reduce noise coupling
  • Input protection on all sensor channels
  • Automotive-rated connectors for vibration resistance
  • Mounting holes aligned with team's standard enclosure

Current Status

The system is in the design phase, with PCB layout complete and ready for manufacturing. Next steps:

  • Completed: Schematic design, component selection, PCB layout, design review with team
  • Next: PCB fabrication and assembly, bench testing with simulated sensor inputs
  • Upcoming: Vehicle integration, field testing, firmware tuning based on real-world data

Design Challenges

Key challenges addressed in the design:

  • Electrical Noise: The vehicle's ignition system and unshielded wiring create significant EMI. Input filtering and shielded cables mitigate this.
  • Environmental Durability: The system must survive mud, water, vibration, and impacts. Conformal coating and sealed connectors provide protection.
